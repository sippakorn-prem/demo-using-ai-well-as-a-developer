## Prompting 101 Pattern

Use this structure:

1. Role: who the AI should act as.
2. Context: what the AI needs to know.
3. Task: the exact job.
4. Constraints: limits, safety rules, and what not to do.
5. Format: the shape of the answer.

## Teaching Instructions

- Show a weak prompt first.
- Ask what context is missing.
- Rewrite it using the pattern.
- Explain why each part reduces ambiguity.
- Keep examples grounded in this demo project.
